The Speed Test App is a specialized software tool designed to assess the speed and performance of an internet connection. It provides users with valuable information about their download and upload speeds, as well as other critical network metrics. These apps are particularly popular among internet users who want to ensure they are getting the internet speeds they are paying for from their Internet Service Providers (ISPs).
